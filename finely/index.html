About

Finely is an AI calendar companion for iOS and Android. It connects to your existing calendar to understand the context behind your events — helping you see not just what's scheduled, but why it matters and how your time actually adds up.

Powered by ScribbleSync, our AI engine that turns handwritten calendars and planners into structured data, Finely bridges the gap between how you naturally plan and how digital calendars work.

What Finely does
  • Connects to your existing calendar — Google, Apple, Outlook
  • Understands the context and intent behind each event
  • Captures handwritten schedules via ScribbleSync and syncs them automatically
  • Shows how your time adds up across projects, people, and priorities
  • Learns your patterns to surface what matters most
